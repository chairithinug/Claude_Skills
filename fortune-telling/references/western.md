# Western — Moon sign and retrogrades

The most useful Western signals for daily/specific-question readings, when the data is known.

## Moon sign today

The Moon changes signs every 2–3 days. Today's Moon sign sets the dominant emotional weather. Quick reference:

| Moon sign | Energy |
|---|---|
| Aries | Energetic, impatient, headfirst |
| Taurus | Steady, sensual, slow-and-deliberate |
| Gemini | Chatty, scattered, curious |
| Cancer | Sensitive, homey, protective |
| Leo | Bold, performative, generous |
| Virgo | Precise, critical, service-oriented |
| Libra | Social, indecisive, harmony-seeking |
| Scorpio | Intense, secretive, all-or-nothing |
| Sagittarius | Adventurous, blunt, expansive |
| Capricorn | Ambitious, restrained, structural |
| Aquarius | Detached, inventive, contrarian |
| Pisces | Dreamy, porous, intuitive |

## Moon void-of-course

When the Moon has made its last major aspect in a sign and not yet entered the next one — typically a few hours, occasionally longer. Avoid initiating new things during void-of-course. Routine and review are fine.

The void-of-course window requires an ephemeris to compute precisely; if uncertain, skip mentioning it.

## Mercury retrograde

Three or four times a year, Mercury appears to move backward against the stars for ~3 weeks. Expect comms, tech, travel friction. Standard Western advice: review, refine, reconnect — don't launch.

If today's retrograde status is unknown, say so and skip rather than fake it.

## Other retrogrades (use sparingly)

- **Venus retrograde** (every 1.5 years, ~40 days) — relationship review, old flames returning
- **Mars retrograde** (every 2 years, ~2 months) — energy/action stalls, frustration
- **Outer-planet retrogrades** (Jupiter, Saturn, etc.) — too slow to matter for daily-luck reading; ignore unless asked

## How to use

For a daily luck overview, the Moon sign is the most useful single Western data point — it gives the emotional cast of the day in one phrase. For a specific question, retrogrades give a hard "delay this" signal when relevant.

## When to skip

If today's Moon sign and retrograde status are both unclear, drop Western from this reading. Other traditions are more calendar-deterministic.
